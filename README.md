CreditPath AI is an AI-powered credit risk management system developed as part of the Infosys Springboard Internship 6.0.
The project focuses on predicting loan default risk and delivering personalized recovery strategies, enabling financial institutions to make faster, smarter, and data-driven lending decisions.

Loan defaults continue to challenge the financial sector, contributing to rising Non-Performing Assets (NPAs) and significant monetary losses. Traditional recovery workflows are mostly manual, time-consuming, and non-personalized, making early detection of risky borrowers difficult.

To address this, CreditPath AI leverages advanced machine learning algorithms to analyze borrower demographics, financial behavior, and repayment patterns. The system is designed to support:

Early identification of potential loan defaulters

Optimized and targeted recovery strategies

Stronger credit risk assessment

Data-driven decision-making
